Science Stream

Engineering

Exam: JEE Main, JEE Advanced, MHT-CET

Roadmap: Choose PCM in 11th-12th, prepare for JEE/MHT-CET, pursue B.Tech/B.E.

Types of Engineering Fields:

  • Computer Science Engineering (CSE): Works on software development, app development, algorithms, cybersecurity. Job roles: Software Developer, Data Scientist, Cybersecurity Analyst.

  • Mechanical Engineering: Deals with design and manufacturing of machines. Job roles: Design Engineer, Quality Engineer, Production Manager.

  • Civil Engineering: Focuses on construction of infrastructure like roads, bridges, buildings. Job roles: Site Engineer, Structural Engineer, Project Manager.

  • Electrical Engineering: Concerned with electrical systems and circuits. Job roles: Electrical Design Engineer, Control Engineer, Power Plant Engineer.

  • Electronics & Telecommunication: Covers embedded systems, communication networks, signal processing. Job roles: Network Engineer, System Designer.

  • Information Technology: Focuses on IT systems, software, database management. Job roles: IT Support, Systems Analyst, Web Developer.

  • Biotechnology: Combines biology and tech to create medical/industrial products. Job roles: Biotech Researcher, Lab Technician, Pharma Analyst.

  • Aerospace Engineering: Related to aircraft, rockets, and space tech. Job roles: Aerospace Engineer, Defense R&D.

  • Automobile Engineering: Deals with vehicle design, manufacturing, safety. Job roles: Automotive Engineer, Crash Analyst.
  • Environmental Engineering: Works on pollution control, waste management. Job roles: Environmental Consultant, Sustainability Expert.

Medical

Exam: NEET

Roadmap: Choose PCB in 11th-12th, prepare for NEET, pursue MBBS/BDS/BAMS/BHMS

Popular Medical Careers:

  • Doctor (MBBS): Diagnose and treat illnesses, perform surgeries, prescribe medicine.
  • Dentist (BDS): Specializes in oral health, treats dental issues.
  • Ayurvedic Doctor (BAMS): Uses Ayurvedic medicine practices for treatment.
  • Homeopathic Doctor (BHMS): Treats patients using homeopathy remedies.
  • Veterinarian: Provides healthcare to animals.
  • Physiotherapist: Helps patients recover physical movement post-injury.
  • Pharmacist: Dispenses medicines, works in hospitals or retail pharmacies.
  • Medical Lab Technician: Conducts lab tests for diagnosis support.

Pure Sciences

Exam: CUET, JEST, IISER Aptitude Test

Roadmap: Pursue B.Sc. in Physics/Chemistry/Math/Biology, then M.Sc., research or teaching

Career Options:

  • Research Scientist: Works in labs or institutions conducting experiments and innovations.
  • College Professor: Teaches undergraduate/postgraduate students, involved in academic research.
  • Lab Analyst: Analyzes samples for chemical or biological data.
  • Scientific Officer: Works in organizations like ISRO, DRDO, BARC in scientific R&D roles.

Commerce Stream

Chartered Accountancy (CA)

Exam: CA Foundation, Intermediate, Final

Roadmap: Choose Commerce in 11th-12th, clear CA exams, articleship training

Careers: Auditor, Tax Consultant, CFO, Finance Manager

Company Secretary (CS)

Exam: CSEET, Executive, Professional

Roadmap: Commerce in 11th-12th, clear CS exams, internship

Careers: Legal Advisor, Compliance Officer, Corporate Planner

B.Com & Management

Roadmap: B.Com/BBA, then MBA or M.Com

Careers: Marketing Manager, HR, Business Analyst, Bank PO

Finance & Banking

Roadmap: B.Com/BBA + Certifications like CFA, CFP

Careers: Investment Banker, Financial Analyst, Wealth Manager

Arts Stream

Humanities & Social Sciences

Roadmap: Choose Arts in 11th-12th, pursue BA in subjects like Psychology, Sociology, Political Science

Careers: Counselor, Social Worker, Researcher, Policy Analyst

Law

Exam: CLAT, LSAT

Roadmap: BA-LLB or LLB after graduation

Careers: Lawyer, Judge, Legal Advisor, Corporate Lawyer

Media & Communication

Roadmap: BA in Journalism, Mass Comm, Visual Arts

Careers: Journalist, Content Creator, PR Executive, Anchor

Vocational & Skill-Based Careers

Diploma & ITI Courses

Roadmap: After 10th or 12th, pursue diplomas in fields like Electrical, Mechanical, Fashion, Hospitality

Careers: Technician, Chef, Electrician, Fashion Designer, Hotel Manager

Paramedical

Roadmap: Diploma/BSc in Paramedical fields

Careers: Radiologist, Lab Technician, Physiotherapist, EMT

Creative & Performing Arts

Roadmap: Courses in Music, Dance, Acting, Fine Arts

Careers: Dancer, Actor, Musician, Art Director

Other Unique Careers

Defence Services

Exam: NDA, CDS, AFCAT

Roadmap: Clear respective exams, training at academies

Hospitality & Service Careers

Courses: Hotel Management, Culinary Arts, Tourism

Career Paths:

  • Chef: Works in restaurants, hotels, or as private chefs.
  • Hotel Manager: Manages hotel operations, customer service.
  • Tour Guide: Guides tourists, shares history and stories.
  • Flight Attendant: Ensures in-flight safety and service.

Other Careers

Defense & Civil Services

Exams: NDA, CDS, UPSC CSE, SSC

  • IAS/IPS: Administrative services for the government.
  • Defense Services: Indian Army, Navy, Air Force (via NDA/CDS).
  • Police/Paramilitary: CRPF, BSF, CAPF.

Entrepreneurship & Startups

Path: No fixed degree, but courses in entrepreneurship or management help.

  • Startup Founder: Builds a business or product from scratch.
  • Freelancer: Offers creative/technical services independently.
  • Content Creator: Builds audience on social media platforms for income.
