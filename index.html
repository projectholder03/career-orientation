Science Stream

Engineering

Exam: JEE Main, JEE Advanced, MHT-CET

Roadmap: Choose PCM in 11th-12th, prepare for JEE/MHT-CET, pursue B.Tech/B.E.

Types of Engineering Fields:

  • Computer Science Engineering (CSE)

    Overview: Focuses on software, algorithms, and computer systems.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with PCM + entrance exams (JEE, CET, etc.).

    Future Scope: IT companies, software development, AI, cybersecurity, data science.

  • Mechanical Engineering

    Overview: Deals with design, manufacturing, and maintenance of machines.

    Eligibility: 10+2 PCM + entrance exams.

    Future Scope: Automobile, robotics, aerospace, manufacturing industries.

  • Civil Engineering

    Overview: Involves construction of buildings, roads, bridges, and infrastructure.

    Eligibility: 10+2 PCM + entrance exams.

    Future Scope: Government projects, real estate, infrastructure companies.

  • Electrical Engineering

    Overview: Studies electrical systems, power generation, and transmission.

    Eligibility: 10+2 PCM + entrance exams.

    Future Scope: Power plants, renewable energy, electronics, railways.

  • Electronics & Telecommunication

    Overview: Focus on communication systems, embedded systems, and circuits.

    Eligibility: 10+2 PCM + entrance exams.

    Future Scope: Telecom industry, networking, semiconductor, embedded systems.

  • Information Technology (IT)

    Overview: Covers computer networks, software, and database management.

    Eligibility: 10+2 PCM + entrance exams.

    Future Scope: Web development, IT support, cloud computing, cyber security.

  • Biotechnology

    Overview: Combines biology with technology to create medical and industrial products.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB/PCM + entrance exams.

    Future Scope: Pharma, agriculture, genetics, medical research.

  • Aerospace Engineering

    Overview: Deals with design and development of aircraft and spacecraft.

    Eligibility: 10+2 PCM + entrance exams.

    Future Scope: Aviation industry, ISRO, DRDO, defense research.

  • Automobile Engineering

    Overview: Focuses on vehicle design, testing, and production.

    Eligibility: 10+2 PCM + entrance exams.

    Future Scope: Automobile companies, R&D, safety testing.

  • Environmental Engineering

    Overview: Works on pollution control, waste management, and sustainability.

    Eligibility: 10+2 PCM + entrance exams.

    Future Scope: NGOs, government agencies, consulting, renewable energy projects.

    • Doctor (MBBS)

    Overview: Diagnose and treat illnesses, prescribe medicines, perform surgeries.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB + NEET exam.

    Future Scope: Hospitals, clinics, government health services, private practice, specialization (MD/MS).

    Dentist (BDS)

    Overview: Specializes in oral health and dental treatment.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB + NEET exam.

    Future Scope: Dental clinics, hospitals, orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry.

    Ayurvedic Doctor (BAMS)

    Overview: Uses Ayurveda (traditional Indian medicine) for treatment.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB + NEET exam.

    Future Scope: Ayurvedic hospitals, wellness centers, private practice, research.

    Homeopathic Doctor (BHMS)

    Overview: Provides treatment using homeopathic remedies.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB + NEET exam.

    Future Scope: Clinics, government hospitals, private consultation, teaching.

    Veterinarian

    Overview: Provides medical care for animals.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB + NEET exam (for BVSc & AH).

    Future Scope: Animal hospitals, zoos, pet clinics, research labs.

    Physiotherapist

    Overview: Helps patients recover movement and strength after injury or illness.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB + entrance test for BPT.

    Future Scope: Rehabilitation centers, sports clinics, hospitals, private practice.

    Pharmacist

    Overview: Prepares and dispenses medicines, ensures safe drug use.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB/PCM + entrance for B.Pharm.

    Future Scope: Retail pharmacy, hospitals, drug manufacturing, clinical research.

    Medical Lab Technician

    Overview: Conducts lab tests to help in diagnosis and treatment.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB + diploma/degree in MLT.

    Future Scope: Hospitals, diagnostic labs, pathology, medical research centers.

    Pure Sciences

    Exam: CUET, JEST, IISER Aptitude Test

    Roadmap: Pursue B.Sc. in Physics/Chemistry/Math/Biology, then M.Sc., research or teaching

    Career Options:

    • Research Scientist: Works in labs or institutions conducting experiments and innovations.
    • College Professor: Teaches undergraduate/postgraduate students, involved in academic research.
    • Lab Analyst: Analyzes samples for chemical or biological data.
    • Scientific Officer: Works in organizations like ISRO, DRDO, BARC in scientific R&D roles.

    Commerce Stream

    Chartered Accountancy (CA)

    Exam: CA Foundation, Intermediate, Final

    Eligibility: 10+2 with Commerce/Non-Commerce stream

    Roadmap: Choose Commerce in 11th-12th, clear CA exams, undergo 3-year articleship training

    Future Scope: Auditor, Tax Consultant, CFO, Finance Manager, Financial Analyst, Entrepreneur

    Company Secretary (CS)

    Exam: CSEET, Executive, Professional

    Eligibility: 10+2 with Commerce/Non-Commerce stream

    Roadmap: Commerce in 11th-12th, clear CS exams, complete internships

    Future Scope: Legal Advisor, Compliance Officer, Corporate Planner, Company Secretary in private or government organizations

    B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce) & Management

    Eligibility: 10+2 with Commerce/Non-Commerce

    Roadmap: Pursue B.Com/BBA → Optionally MBA, M.Com, or professional certifications (CPA, CFA)

    Future Scope: Marketing Manager, HR Executive, Business Analyst, Accountant, Bank PO, Investment Analyst

    Finance & Banking

    Eligibility: 10+2 with Commerce/Non-Commerce

    Roadmap: B.Com/BBA → Professional Certifications like CFA, CFP, FRM → Jobs in Finance sector

    Future Scope: Investment Banker, Financial Analyst, Wealth Manager, Risk Analyst, Portfolio Manager, Corporate Finance Executive

    Economics

    Eligibility: 10+2 with Commerce/Arts/Science

    Roadmap: Pursue B.A./B.Sc in Economics → M.A. Economics → Jobs in research, government, or corporate sector

    Future Scope: Economist, Policy Analyst, Economic Consultant, Data Analyst, Research Analyst in banks or government agencies

    Accounting & Taxation

    Eligibility: 10+2 with Commerce/Non-Commerce

    Roadmap: Diploma or B.Com with specialization in accounting → Professional courses in Taxation, GST, Tally, or CPA

    Future Scope: Accountant, Tax Consultant, Auditor, Financial Planner, Payroll Manager

    Insurance & Actuarial Science

    Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics

    Roadmap: B.Sc in Actuarial Science / Mathematics → Professional actuarial exams → Jobs in insurance, risk management, pensions

    Future Scope: Actuary, Risk Analyst, Insurance Analyst, Pension Consultant, Investment Analyst

    Arts Stream

    Humanities & Social Sciences

    Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream

    Roadmap: Choose Arts in 11th-12th → Pursue BA in subjects like Psychology, Sociology, Political Science, History → Optionally MA or research programs

    Future Scope: Counselor, Social Worker, Researcher, Policy Analyst, NGO roles, Government or academic positions

    Law

    Exam: CLAT, LSAT, AILET

    Eligibility: 10+2 for integrated BA-LLB, Graduation for LLB

    Roadmap: BA-LLB (5 years) or LLB after graduation → Bar Exam / State Bar Council registration → Specializations (Corporate, Criminal, Intellectual Property, etc.)

    Future Scope: Lawyer, Judge, Legal Advisor, Corporate Lawyer, Public Prosecutor, Legal Consultant

    Media & Communication

    Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream

    Roadmap: Pursue BA in Journalism, Mass Communication, Visual Arts, or Multimedia → Optionally MA, diploma courses, or certifications

    Future Scope: Journalist, Content Creator, PR Executive, Anchor, Social Media Manager, Event Manager, Film/TV Production roles

    Fine Arts & Performing Arts

    Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream

    Roadmap: Bachelor’s in Fine Arts (BFA) or Performing Arts (BPA) → Optionally MA / Diploma / Workshops → Build portfolio

    Future Scope: Painter, Sculptor, Musician, Dancer, Actor, Art Director, Choreographer, Theatre/Film Artist

    Psychology

    Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream

    Roadmap: BA in Psychology → MA / M.Sc Psychology → PhD for research/academic roles

    Future Scope: Clinical Psychologist, Counselor, HR Specialist, Researcher, Forensic Psychologist, Academic roles

    Design & Fashion

    Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream, portfolio may be required

    Roadmap: Bachelor’s in Fashion Design, Interior Design, or Graphic Design → Internships & portfolio building → Master’s or specialized diplomas

    Future Scope: Fashion Designer, Interior Designer, Graphic Designer, Animator, UI/UX Designer, Product Designer

    Vocational & Skill-Based Careers

    Diploma & ITI Courses

    Eligibility: 10th or 12th pass

    Roadmap: After 10th/12th, pursue diplomas or ITI courses in Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, Fashion, Hospitality, or Computer Applications → Gain practical skills and certifications

    Future Scope: Technician, Electrician, Mechanic, Plumber, Fashion Designer, Hotel Manager, IT support, Machine Operator

    Paramedical

    Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB

    Roadmap: Diploma / BSc in Paramedical fields like Radiology, Physiotherapy, Medical Lab Technology, Operation Theatre Technology → Optional specialization courses

    Future Scope: Radiologist Assistant, Lab Technician, Physiotherapist, Emergency Medical Technician (EMT), Rehabilitation Specialist, Hospital Technician

    Creative & Performing Arts

    Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream, aptitude tests or portfolio may be required

    Roadmap: Courses in Music, Dance, Acting, Fine Arts, Theatre, Film Studies → Participate in performances, exhibitions, internships → Optionally pursue Masters or certifications

    Future Scope: Dancer, Actor, Musician, Choreographer, Painter, Sculptor, Art Director, Theatre/Film Artist, Content Creator

    Hospitality & Tourism

    Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream

    Roadmap: Diploma / Bachelor’s in Hotel Management, Culinary Arts, Travel & Tourism → Internships in hotels, restaurants, or travel agencies

    Future Scope: Hotel Manager, Chef, Tour Guide, Event Manager, Travel Consultant, Cruise Ship Manager

    IT & Digital Skills

    Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream

    Roadmap: Short-term courses or diplomas in Web Development, Digital Marketing, Graphic Design, Data Analytics, or Cybersecurity → Build portfolio and freelance experience

    Future Scope: Web Developer, Digital Marketer, Graphic Designer, Data Analyst, Cybersecurity Analyst, Freelancer, Startup opportunities

    Sports & Fitness Careers

    Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream

    Roadmap: Diploma / Bachelor’s in Physical Education, Sports Science, Yoga, or Coaching → Participate in training, workshops, and certifications

    Future Scope: Fitness Trainer, Yoga Instructor, Sports Coach, Physiotherapist, Nutritionist, Professional Athlete, Sports Analyst

    Other Unique Careers

    Defence Services

    Exams: NDA, CDS, AFCAT

    Eligibility: 10+2 for NDA, Graduation for CDS/AFCAT, Age criteria as per exam

    Roadmap: Clear the respective exam → Training at military/air/naval academy → Commissioned as officer

    Future Scope: Indian Army, Navy, Air Force officer, Specialized defense roles, Leadership positions, Strategic planning

    Hospitality & Service Careers

    Courses: Hotel Management, Culinary Arts, Tourism, Travel & Tourism

    Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream

    Roadmap: Diploma/Bachelor’s in Hotel Management, Culinary Arts, or Tourism → Internships in hotels, resorts, restaurants, or travel agencies → Gain experience

    Future Scope:

    • Chef: Works in restaurants, hotels, resorts, or as private chef
    • Hotel Manager: Manages hotel operations, staff, and guest satisfaction
    • Tour Guide: Guides tourists, shares history, and manages tours
    • Flight Attendant: Ensures in-flight safety, service, and passenger comfort
    • Event Manager: Plans and executes corporate or personal events

    Entrepreneurship & Startups

    Eligibility: No fixed degree required; knowledge in business or technical skills is helpful

    Roadmap: Identify market opportunity → Build a business plan → Secure funding or bootstrap → Launch startup → Scale operations

    Future Scope: Startup Founder, Freelancer, Consultant, Social Entrepreneur, Business Innovator

    Other Careers

    Defense & Civil Services

    Exams: NDA, CDS, UPSC CSE, SSC

    Eligibility: 10+2 for NDA, Graduation for CDS/UPSC/SSC, Age criteria as per exam

    Roadmap: Prepare for Preliminary Exam → Main Exam → Interview → Training at respective academies

    Future Scope: IAS/IPS Officer, Defense Officer (Army/Navy/Air Force), Police/Paramilitary (CRPF, BSF, CAPF), Administrative Services, Policy Making

    Entrepreneurship & Startups

    Eligibility: No fixed degree; courses in entrepreneurship or management help

    Roadmap: Identify market opportunity → Build business plan → Secure funding or bootstrap → Launch startup → Scale operations

    Future Scope: Startup Founder, Freelancer, Consultant, Content Creator, Social Entrepreneur, Business Innovator

    Creative & Digital Careers

    Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream; skill-based certifications recommended

    Roadmap: Pursue courses in Graphic Design, Digital Marketing, Web Development, Animation, or Content Creation → Build portfolio and network → Freelancing, employment, or startup

    Future Scope: Content Creator, Graphic Designer, Animator, YouTuber, Social Media Manager, Digital Marketing Specialist

    Pilot

    Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics & Math, Age: 17–30, Medical fitness required

    Roadmap: Obtain Commercial Pilot License → Training at flying school → Join airline or private aviation company

    Future Scope: Captain, Flight Instructor, Airline Pilot, Aviation Manager

    Research & Innovation

    Eligibility: Graduation/Bachelor’s in relevant science, tech, or social science field

    Roadmap: Pursue higher studies (M.Sc/M.Tech/PhD) → Join research labs, universities, or think tanks → Publish and innovate

    Future Scope: Research Scientist, Innovator, Policy Analyst, Academic Researcher, Product Developer
